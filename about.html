About Smart Crop Advisory

Who We Are

Smart Crop Advisory is an intelligent platform designed to help farmers and agricultural enthusiasts identify crop diseases quickly and accurately. By simply uploading or capturing a photo of your crop, our system uses advanced image recognition technology to detect possible diseases and provide effective solutions.

Our mission is to empower farmers with the right tools to protect their crops, improve yield, and reduce losses. Combining the power of Artificial Intelligence with practical agricultural knowledge, Smart Crop Advisory serves as a bridge between modern technology and traditional farming.
